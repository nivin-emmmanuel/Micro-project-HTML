The Alchemist

 

Published on: October 14, 2005

Author: Paulo Coelho

Publisher: AD &Co

Number of pages: 300

Genres: fiction

 

This novel has inspired people all around the world. It is all about a shepherd boy named Santiago who travels from his homeland in Spain to Egyptian desert in search of treasure buried in the Pyramids. No one knows what the treasure is, but what starts as a journey to find worldly goods turns into the discovery of treasure found within. The story of santiago is an eternal testement to the transforming power of our dreams and the importance of listening to our hearts...
